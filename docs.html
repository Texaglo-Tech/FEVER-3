What is Texaglo

Texaglo is a Decentralized Research Organiazation That builds tools and open source solutions to problems the community faces. Over the last two years we have created these tools to help the network create Web3 monetized Content easier and faster. Here are the tools that the community has requested and we have built:

Content and Portals

Portals are Deployed Content that Members can create and share on various platforms. There are 7 portal types available currently that users can create: Token Gates, Forms, Mint pages, Allowlist, Websites, Music playlists, Tickets

Art Generators

Art Generators are the backbone of generative collections for creating communities tied to handrawn artwork. We have built a webtool that allows the community to create tokenized artwork without code

Payment Splitters

Payment Splitters are programs that split payments automatically when assets are sent to the program. You can use the open source payment splitter developed for Teams and Organizations who wanted to build in Payment Transparency and Trust.

Data

Content can Generate Data that can be exported by owners and used in anyway that they want. Data is only accessable to the owner of the account. You will also be able to share the information with others soon Roadmap

Monetization

You Can Monetize the content you create by charging for access in Crypto Currencies or in USD. We have integrated Three solutions to achieve this which are Solana and Cross Mint

Branding

We Made Tooling to allow you to fully customize your portal from the seo to the text and button colors. This is yours so you can express yourself however you want with our Portal editing Page

Protocol structure

Let's talk about what's under the hood

We have put this technology together for you in a way that is useful to the community and structured in a way that is consistant and repeatable

Protocol Hierarchy
  • Expand all
  • Collapse all
  • Art - The protocol is grounded in the ability to create and share art
    • gen.glo - These programs create layered assets and their associated metadata in preperation for creating nft on etherium or solana
    • upload.glo - The upload program sends your bundled payments to arweave along with your artwork to create the metadata and images permanently
    • Mint.glo - The Mint Program Deploys an Candy-machine-Mint from Metaplex and asscoicates the mint and the uploaded images and Metadata
  • Payments - Payments are the clue that keeps teams together so we baked this into the protocol with our payment infrastructure
    • split.glo - This program allows you to create splits to enforce payment arangements and increase trust in teams
    • pay.glo - This program allows products to accept Tokens and Fiat with Crossmint
    • owner.glo - This program routes payments from portals to the current owner account
    • allow.glo - This program controls access to content. Specifically who can access what content and when they can access it
  • Accounts - Accounts allow for the the management of all assets on the protocol and determin who recieves payment
    • account.glo - Members Mint accounts on Devnet or Mainnet to access Content as a member or Contributer
    • transfer.glo - This program manages the transfer of accounts and routes payments and other assets associated to the new account owner
  • Content - Content created is shared online and is accessable everywhere
    • create.glo - Portals are how people interact with your content.There are 7 portal types that you can create
    • edit.glo - Edit your portals with this program. The program checks ownership before editing is allowed on portals
    • deploy.glo - Deploy your portals. The owner of the account is the only one that can deploy content
  • Distribution - The application is hosted currently hosted in a hybrid onchain and offchain system
    • offchain.glo - This program allows you to utilize highspeed offchain distribution infrastructure
    • onchain.glo - This program allows you to utilize the onchain distribution Methods

FAQ

Begin typing your question. If we don't have an answer for it in our FAQ, please leave us a message on our contact page.

  • What is Texaglo?

    Texaglo is a Decentralized Research Organization that develops tools for artists, creators, and developers. It is also a website where these tools are hosted and made available for use.

  • What is Bump?

    Bump is an open-source Dapp (Decentralized Application) hosted on Texaglo. It can be installed on your web server or used through an account to create community experiences, gated content, digital products, or communicate with other creators in the Texaglo Network. Initially designed as a tool for artists, Glo has evolved into a full-fledged Content Monetization System (CMS), capable of powering websites, networks, and communities.

  • Why choose Bump

    Choosing Bump empowers you with control and flexibility. Unlike traditional platforms, Glo offers an easy way to participate in Web3 through user-friendly forms and drag-and-drop tools. It is self-hosted, ensuring data ownership and privacy. Additionally, Glo is simple to install and configure, without the need for extensive file editing or template customization.

  • What are Bump's features?

    Bump boasts a wide range of features including creating mint experiences, forms, decentralized websites, monetized digital assets, and much more. It is constantly evolving, with new features being added to enhance its capabilities.

  • When was Bump first released?

    Bump was initially released in 2021 as a fork of Hashlips/artgenerator by Hashlips or Daniel Botha.

  • What is the Blockchain?

    Blockchain is a decentralized ledger technology that records transactions across multiple computers. This ensures that the data is secure and cannot be altered retroactively. It is widely used in cryptocurrencies, smart contracts, and decentralized applications.

  • What is an NFT?

    NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token. It is a type of digital asset that represents ownership or proof of authenticity of a unique item using blockchain technology. NFTsare commonly used for digital art, collectibles, music, and other unique content.

  • How is Glo related to other Content applications?

    Bump, hosted on Texaglo, is primarily focused on monetizing content for creators. It was inspired by Linktree and the challenges faced by small business owners who desired features that would enable them to conduct business in Web3. Bump provides creators with tools to publish content, engage with communities, and monetize their work through blockchain technology.

  • Do I need to know Code to use Texaglo?

    No, you do not need to know how to code to use Texaglo and Bump. The platform is designed to be user-friendly, allowing you to create and manage content through a graphical user interface. However, for advanced customization or integration of certain plugins, some knowledge of coding may be beneficial.

  • How does Bump integrate with the Solana blockchain?

    Bump integrates with the Solana blockchain to enable fast and secure transactions. Solana's high throughput and low fees make it an ideal blockchain for creating and trading NFTs and other digital assets. Through Bump, creators can mint NFTs, engage in decentralized finance (DeFi) activities, and leverage the benefits of blockchain technology in content monetization.
